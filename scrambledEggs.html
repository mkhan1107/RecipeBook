SCRAMBLED EGGS

A brekfast classic. Both quick and easy to make!!!

INGREDIENTS

  • milk
  • eggs x 3
  • butter

STEPS

  1. crack three eggs into a bowl
  2. add a table spoon of milk
  3. whisk eggs
  4. put a pan on the stove and heat it on medium
  5. spread butter on the pan
  6. pour the bowl on the pan and continually stir it
  7. once the eggs look half done, take the pan off the stove and put into a bowl
  8. ENJOY!!!
