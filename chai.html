CHAI

The drink of South Asia

INGREDIENTS

  • Orange Pekoe Teabag x 3
  • Milk
  • Water

STEPS

  1. add half a cup of water to a pot
  2. heat the pot until the water boils
  3. add the teabags
  4. once the water changes color add the milk
  5. once the chai rises it is done
  6. pour and enjoy!!!
